Ingredients

  • Plain / all purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Milk, full fat
  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Whip the eggs and sugar
  2. Gradually add flour
  3. Hot melted butter in milk
  4. Mix some batter into hot milk
  5. Slowly pour milk mixture back into whipped eggs
  6. Pour into 2 cake pans lined with parchment/baking paper.
  7. Knock out big air bubbles
  8. Bake 30 minutes
  9. COOL upside down
  10. Decorate
